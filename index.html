Understanding AI: A Conceptual Approach
Chapter 2, Lesson 2.1: The Old Way - Programming Rules

Lesson 2.1 is a foundational Lesson Day introducing students to traditional programming through relatable examples. Students learn how computers follow explicit, step-by-step instructions and explore the strengths and limitations of rule-based programming. This lesson emphasizes logical thinking, procedural reasoning, understanding deterministic systems, and recognizing the boundary between what can and cannot be explicitly programmed‚Äîcompetencies addressed across multiple state and national standards frameworks.
