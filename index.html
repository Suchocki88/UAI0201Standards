Lesson 2.1: The Old Way - Programming Rules

Chapter: 2
Type: Lesson Day
Duration: 50 minutes

Lesson Focus: Understanding traditional programming through simple examples. Students learn how humans write step-by-step instructions for computers, exploring the power and fundamental limitations of explicit rule-based systems.
